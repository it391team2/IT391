Our Mission

ScannoKart is a comprehensive money-spent and expiration date monitoring application designed to bring intelligence to your household inventory. We believe that managing your pantry shouldn't be a chore, and knowing exactly what you have should be as simple as a single scan.

Financial Tracking

Stop wondering where your grocery budget goes. ScannoKart automatically tracks the total amount of money spent on your groceries, providing insights into your spending habits.

event_busy

Expiration Monitoring

Eliminate food waste with our smart monitoring system. By tracking the shelf life of every item, we provide timely alerts before your food expires.

shopping_cart

Smart Restocking

Never run out of the essentials again. Our system monitors consumption and notifies you exactly when it's time to get more.

The Total Inventory Solution

In a world where food waste accounts for significant financial loss, ScannoKart serves as your digital pantry guardian, helping you maintain an efficient, cost-effective household.
